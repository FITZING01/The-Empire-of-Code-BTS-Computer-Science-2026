# Complete Pedagogical Guide: Objective 19.5

## Introduction
This guide is designed to accompany you in your final BTS preparation. It synthesizes the pitfalls to avoid and the Senior quality standards expected by examiners.

---

## 1. Strategy for the Written Part (10 points)
- **Precision of terms**: Do not confuse HTML (structure) and CSS (style).
- **URL Components**: Always cite the protocol, domain, port, and path.
- **Marketing**: Know how to justify why digital is supplanting traditional (cost, measurement, targeting).

---

## 2. Strategy for SQL (10 points)
- **Subject 1 (Flights)**: Pay attention to triple joins (Pilot-Flight-Plane). Always use `DISTINCT` if asked for "Names" without duplicates.
- **Subject 2 (Sales)**: Master collective comparison operators (`ALL`, `ANY`) and aggregation functions in subqueries.
- **Format**: Always end queries with a semicolon.

---

## 3. Strategy for Web (10 points)
- **Subject 1 (Jerseys)**: The focus is on dynamic display via JS.
- **Subject 2 (School)**: The focus is on complex form validation (Neighborhood, Phone).
- **UI Standard**: Use Bootstrap for an instant professional appearance.

---

## 4. Strategy for UML & Java (10 points)
- **UML**: Respect inheritance arrows (empty triangle) and association arrows. Do not forget multiplicities (1..*, 0..1).
- **Java (Article)**: Exception handling is crucial. The use of `protected` shows mastery of inheritance.
- **Java (Account)**: Financial calculations must be precise (use `double`).

---

## Conclusion
Success relies on the ability to switch between the two contexts (Aviation vs. Commercial). This sprint trains you for this mental gymnastics.
