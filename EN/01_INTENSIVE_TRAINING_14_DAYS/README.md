# SPRINT BTS OBJECTIVE 19.5 - ECM PROGRAMMING

## Presentation
This folder contains the intensive training program for the BTS E-Commerce and Digital Marketing (ECM) practical programming exam. It has been entirely redesigned to cover **100% of the requirements of the two official subjects**:
- **Subject 1**: Airline Company (UML/SQL) & Football Jersey Marketplace (Web) & Account Management (Java).
- **Subject 2**: Sales Management (SQL) & School Supplies Marketplace (Web) & Invoice/Article Management (Java).

---

## Program Structure
1.  **Day 01: Fundamental Theory** (Definitions, Web, Digital Marketing, B2B/B2C).
2.  **Day 02 & 03: Expert SQL** (Joins, Subqueries, Aggregations, Data Manipulation).
3.  **Day 04 to 06: Static & Dynamic Web** (PHP, JS, Bootstrap, LocalStorage).
4.  **Day 07: Web Assembly & Validation**.
5.  **Day 08 & 09: UML Design** (Use Cases, Sequence, Classes).
6.  **Day 10 & 11: Java Object-Oriented Programming** (Encapsulation, Inheritance, Exceptions).
7.  **Day 12 & 13: Mock Exams** (Full Corrections Subject 1 & Subject 2).
8.  **Day 14: Optimization & Hacks**.

---

## Performance Objectives
- Master the transition from UML analysis to Java/SQL implementation.
- Create responsive and functional e-commerce interfaces in less than 6 hours.
- Pass the theoretical part (10 points) without any mistakes.
- Obtain a minimum grade of **19.5/20**.
